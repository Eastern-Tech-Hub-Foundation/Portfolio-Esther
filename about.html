A refined data analysist, a mentor and a well experienced administrator with a good communication and interpersonal skills.

Esther Ahamenu is an engaging and hard-working administrator with a proven track record in managing and controlling data and records, complemented by foundational skills in data analysis. She possesses strong organizational and leadership capabilities, adept at managing resources efficiently and effectively. Esther is a good team player, known for her excellent interpersonal and communication skills, and her ability to handle multiple tasks simultaneously with keen attention to detail and skilled decision-making.

Esther's educational background includes a BSc. in Business Information Systems from Koforidua Technical University, where she is currently enrolled, and a Diploma in Business Administration from the same institution. Her work experience spans several roles, including her current position as a volunteer administrator at Eastern Tech Hub in Koforidua. In this role, she has been instrumental in organizing conferences, coordinating interviews, and ensuring the availability of essential logistics. During her national service at Koforidua Technical University, she excelled as an administrative assistant, managing documents, maintaining a proper filing system, and preparing financial records. She also gained valuable experience as an intern at Municipal Safeway Co-operative Credit Union, where she assisted with cash receipts, managed records, and conducted educational presentations.

In addition to her administrative roles, Esther has participated in several notable projects and research initiatives. At Eastern Tech Hub, she contributed to the Jabari Project by conducting a community needs assessment survey. She also analyzed seminarians' perspectives on justice and peace, providing valuable recommendations to foster harmony within the seminary. Furthermore, Esther played a key role in the OurCity Project by gathering and documenting data from various organizations to propose the development of a unified website platform. Her diverse skill set includes document and file management, data analysis, project coordination, communication, documentation, and record-keeping, as well as problem-solving.
